Design Principles

Deliberately Design

Empower the user to quickly accomplish goals by implementing an interface that is light, responsive, and focused on primary tasks. Make sure every element is there for a reason. Deliberately use clean typography and simple animations to keep the work flow fluid, giving the user a perception of performance and synergy.

  • Clean Design – Appropriate white space to place focus on the subject
  • Liberated Layout – Thin lines and white space to break up the layout, not to create containers
  • Smart Iconography – Designed to scale and used sparingly to enhance usability
  • Layered Features – Less relevant features are accessible but not in the way

Make It Human

Move beyond the ‘usable’ interface to a more intuitive, human interface; one designed to naturally flow with the user’s tendencies and that has the user’s needs as the primary focus. Communicate openly and humanly to engage and inform the user. Make our users the heroes by providing a product tailored to their needs and goals. Our products don’t do amazing things; our users do amazing things with our products

  • Systematic Flow – Organized by importance of tasks and user expectations
  • Friendly Voice – Communicated as if the user is a respected colleague or friend
  • Intuitive Interface – Make it easy to understand, easy to access, easy to use
  • User Affinity – Know our users; every decision we make should be made with our users in mind

Focus On Craftsmanship

Create a product that you would want to use, your excitement and pride will shine through. Inspire confidence and comfort in the user by keeping the product voice, interface, and processes consistent. Present innovative ways to accomplish the user’s tasks. By doing so, you show commitment to the user and their needs.

  • Impeccable Detail – Attention to detail projects quality and professionalism
  • Consistent Experience – Use familiar UI patterns, tools, and concepts as well as conventions and architecture across the portfolio
  • Responsive Framework – Beautiful design and functionality persists across all OS and platforms
  • Innovative Solutions – Imagine new and creative solutions and bring them to the table, never stop innovating
